Privacy Policy

MARIbelajar · Effective date: 7 July 2026 · Last updated: 12 July 2026

The short version. MARIbelajar is a mathematics learning app for primary‑school children. It works fully offline. We do not run accounts, we do not show ads, we do not track you, and we do not collect analytics. Your child's name, avatar and learning progress stay on the device. The only information that leaves the device is what is strictly needed to (a) download the handwriting recognition model once, and (b) process a subscription purchase through the App Store or Google Play.

This policy explains what information the MARIbelajar app ("the App", "we", "us") handles and how. The App is published by an independent app developer based in Malaysia ("the Developer"). If you have any questions, contact us at maribelajar.app@gmail.com.

1. Information stored on your device (not collected by us)

When you create a profile, the App stores the following only on the device, using the operating system's local storage. It is never transmitted to the Developer:

  • A profile name or nickname and a chosen avatar (you decide what to enter — a nickname is fine).
  • Learning progress: which questions/chapters have been attempted, quiz history, Math Drill and Number Rush scores.
  • App settings: sounds, haptics, quiz feedback mode.

You can remove this data at any time by deleting the profile inside the App (Settings → profiles), or by uninstalling the App. Deleting a profile erases that profile's progress from the device.

2. Information processed by third parties

a. Subscriptions (RevenueCat + Apple / Google)

If you choose to subscribe, the purchase is processed by Apple App Store or Google Play and managed through RevenueCat, which we use to verify and restore subscriptions. For this, a randomly‑generated anonymous app‑user ID and your purchase receipt / subscription status are processed. This data is not linked to your identity by us and is not used to track you across apps or websites. See the RevenueCat Privacy Policy, Apple Privacy Policy and Google Privacy Policy.

b. Handwriting recognition (Google ML Kit — Number Rush only)

The optional Number Rush game recognises digits you write by hand using Google's on‑device ML Kit Digital Ink. The recognition model is downloaded once over the internet, after which recognition happens entirely on the device. Your handwriting is not sent to Google or to us. As part of downloading and managing this model, Google's ML Kit service may send a non‑identifying device identifier and basic diagnostics to Google; see the Google Privacy Policy. If the model cannot be downloaded, the game falls back to an on‑screen number pad.

c. Crash reporting

We do not embed a third‑party analytics or crash SDK. Basic, aggregated crash information may be provided to us by Apple and Google through their standard developer dashboards if you have opted in to share diagnostics with them at the OS level; this is governed by Apple's and Google's policies.

3. What we do NOT do

  • No accounts, logins, or email sign‑up.
  • No advertising and no advertising identifiers.
  • No third‑party analytics or user tracking; no data sold or shared for marketing.
  • No access to precise location, contacts, camera, microphone, or photos.

4. Children's privacy

MARIbelajar is designed for children and is distributed in Malaysia. We handle personal data in line with Malaysia's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) and follow international best practice for children's apps (the principles behind COPPA and GDPR‑K). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children on any server — the App has no server that receives child data; all learning data stays on the device. Purchases are protected by a parental gate (a simple check intended for an adult) before any payment, restore, or external link. Parents and guardians can review or delete on‑device data by managing profiles in the App.

5. Data retention & deletion

Because on‑device data never reaches us, we retain nothing to delete on our side. On the device, data persists until you delete the profile or uninstall the App. Subscription/receipt data held by Apple, Google and RevenueCat is retained per their respective policies.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct or delete personal data. Since we hold no personal data about you on our systems, the practical way to exercise these rights is to manage or delete profiles on the device, and to manage your subscription in your App Store / Google Play account. For any request, contact maribelajar.app@gmail.com.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected here with a new "Last updated" date.
