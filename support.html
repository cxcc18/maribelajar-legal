Frequently asked questions

How do I restore my subscription on a new device?

Open Settings in the app → Manage subscription → Restore purchases. Purchases are tied to your App Store / Google Play account.

Is my child's data collected?

No account is needed and all learning progress stays on the device. See the Privacy Policy for details.

How do I cancel the subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by the App Store / Google Play — you can cancel any time in your store account settings.
